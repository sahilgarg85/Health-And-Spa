Pre-Wedding Rituals

by Admin | 15 March, 2020 | Pre-Wedding, Rituals | 21 Comments
Calling all brides out there! We know you all want to look and feel your best on your wedding. While your natural beauty will radiate through the roof on that day, a few pre-wedding beauty rituals will give you the extra oomph and confidence you might need.

Let’s get you ready from head to toe!

Facials



Yes, you’ll be in the spotlight with all eyes on you. Worry not! Our range of facial treatments will give you the perfect glow that’ll make heads turn as you make your grand entrance.


  • Shine Facial

    Our shine facial treatment enriched with essential skin vitamins will enliven your day and make your skin supple with an everlasting radiance. In this treatment, the therapist will also relax your facial muscles and enhance your blood circulation with gentle strokes.

  • Young & Radiant Facial

    With this tailor-made facial, we ensure that your beauty knows no age. Our Young & Radiant facial for mature or neglected skin infuses the skin with scientific ingredients proven to slow down the aging process, repair damaged skin and restore freshness back into your skin. The result? Younger-looking radiant skin.

Body Polishing



While you give your face all the attention don’t forget about your body. Body polishing will give the rest of your body a glow just like your face. This treatment will exfoliate and hydrate your skin to get that smooth, soft and radiant finish.


  • Floral Mist Polisher

    We bring to you a misty fusion of aromatic oils, Palash, saffron and special floral extracts from the Himalayan valley. This gentle exfoliating body polisher evens out your skin tone leaving it soft, supple and giving it that dewy glow.

  • Vanilla & Nutmeg Smoothie

    A luxurious creamy treatment that leaves your skin soft. This aromatic mixture provides essential hydration and conditions the damaged skin leaving your body with a delicate sweet fragrance and velvety feel.

Full Body Massage



After running all around and perfecting your dance moves for the Sangeet, you definitely need some me-time to de-stress, relax and get back on your feet for the big day. A full body massage is a perfect reward for all your wedding planning efforts.


  • Swedish Massage

    This traditional technique works with light to medium pressure with long kneading & gliding strokes. It will reinforce your oxygen level in the blood, enhance blood circulation and ease out the wedding stress.

  • Reflexology

    Focusing on different pressure points of reflexes, our healers work carefully on the reflex zones of your tired feet, balancing the energy flow in your body. We guarantee you’ll leave our premises with a new spring in your step!

  • Head Massage

    Boost your clarity of thought and concentration power by the traditional head massage. It releases stress as tension is eased out of tight muscles in the head and upper neck region. Our signature brew of hair nourishing herbal oil will invigorate the body, balance the Chakras and infuse a sense of deep calm and relaxation.

  • Lymphatic Face Massage

    A lymphatic face massage is a soothing and natural therapeutic treatment that drains toxins from the face by using a gentle technique of rhythmic strokes to stimulate and increase the flow of lymph fluids. This relaxing massage is done around the eyes, behind the ears, at the bottom of the head and neck area, which is where the lymph nodes are located. It is the perfect treatment for skincare and acne.

Hair Spa



Your dream of being goldy locks has finally come true and we all know how good hair can speak louder than words. A hair spa is just what you need to be the best tressed! Our Amazon Almond hair spa will retain the moisture and help you get rid of dry frizzy hair. It will also naturally increase the elasticity of your hair and restore it’s shine giving luscious bouncy locks


Mani And Pedi



And lastly, the finishing touches! Your hands and feet are also going to be the focal point with that big beautiful diamond rock on your finger or the aesthetic mehndi design. Our spa manicure and pedicure service go beyond the regular cleaning, cutting and shaping of your delicate nails. Our organic products breathe new life into your nails and skin. Our healers will advise you on a manicure/pedicure session depending on your specific skin type.
